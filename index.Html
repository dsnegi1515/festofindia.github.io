Festivals In India

Diwali Dusherra Holi

There are many festivals in India , but most popular festivals are Holi , Diwali and Dushhera. These festivals have different storys and also are divided in days. Like Diwali is divided in 5 days First day is Dhan Teras , Second day is Bhai Dooj Third day is Chhoti Diwali Forth day is Diwali Last but not the least is Goverdhan Pooja.
